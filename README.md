# 📈 Growth Efficiency Audit Dashboard (Excel)

## 🚀 Overview
This project is an executive-style business performance audit built in Excel to evaluate whether a company scaled revenue efficiently from **January to August 2025**.

The goal was to determine whether growth was **healthy and sustainable**, or if revenue was increasing due to **inefficient marketing spend and operational breakdowns**.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem
Leadership needs to understand:

- Is the business scaling efficiently?
- Is marketing spend translating into profitable growth?
- Are there any operational anomalies hurting performance?
- Is traffic growth actually converting into revenue?

This project answers those questions through KPI development, trend analysis, anomaly detection, and executive reporting.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics Developed
The following business KPIs were created in Excel:

- **Net Revenue**
- **Marketing Spend**
- **ROI**
- **CPA**
- **Month-on-Month Revenue Growth**
- **Month-on-Month Marketing Spend Growth**
- **Refund Tracking**
- **Traffic vs Revenue Relationship**

---

## 🚨 Key Findings

### 🔻 Event 1 — April Efficiency Breakdown During Scaling
- Marketing Spend increased **+68.63%**
- Net Revenue increased only **+18.14%**
- ROI dropped from **4.91 to 3.44 (~30% decline)**

**Probable Cause:**  
Rapid spend scaling likely reduced traffic quality and conversion efficiency.

---

### 🔻 Event 2 — June Operational Stress Signal
- Refunds spiked from ~**16K to 77K (+376%)**
- Net Revenue declined **-8.88%**
- ROI weakened from **4.53 to 4.12**

**Probable Cause:**  
Potential operational stress, product quality issue, campaign-target mismatch, or fulfillment breakdown.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insight
Although revenue increased significantly from January to August, **efficiency did not improve materially**.

This suggests that the business was able to scale revenue, but **without proportionate improvement in marketing return or operational stability**.
